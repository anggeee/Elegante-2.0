about Elegante
Elegante is an android application and website viewing platform for users who enjoys looking at different styles of trendy clothes at an affordable price. It also offers variation of clothes for men, women and kids.
Elegante's mobile app takes personalization to greater levels. Elegante's comprehensive information is available in the application. It also enables resellers to register and set up their own online store/upload their products. It serves not only resellers but also end customers.
Get the most out of Elegante by adding to cart, browsing, and selling! Now available on the Google Play Store.
